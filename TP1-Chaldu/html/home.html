TP 1 - FARMACIA

Meds

In the Meds menu you can create different Meds by filling in the fields respective to a Medicament. The fields include ID, Name, Price and Stock.

Sales

In the Sales menu you can make sales, and also see the list of different sales that have been made in the past.

Inventory

In the inventory menu you can see the sales made and also see the different medicaments that have already been created.
